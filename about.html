About Basketball

General



The game of basketball is split into 2 main components:

  • Offence
  • Defence

General Rules to Know



When playing basketball there is a set rules that you need to follow in order to play according to the rules. Some of these rules include:

  • Dribbling
  • Areas in which you score
  • How many people can play at once
  • Fouls

Facts About Basketball



The NBA (national basketball association) is a league where the best male players around the world are drafted onto teams that are located in different places in the USA and Canada. The average salary of an NBA player is 9.6 Million dollars as the viewership and merchandise that helps keep the NBA up allows for higher pay for the roster of players for a specific team. Recently one of the most renowned players ever Lebron James broke the scoring record set by Kareem Abdul-Jabbar at 38,387 making Lebron the highest scoring player in NBA history.
